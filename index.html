The GridBlock is a temporary plug

which allows any in-field pool service technician cleaning a DE filter the means to quickly and easily eliminate a damaged grid from the manifold and still be able to re-assemble the filter for immediate use.

In many cases, technicians are caught off guard

when they’re already on-site and need to clean a pool or spa DE filter due to high pressure/low flow or evidence of DE in the pool and don’t always have replacement filter grids with them.

If one or two of the grids are visibly damaged

after the filter has been opened, disassembled and cleaned, the GridBlock can be inserted into the manifold to “block” the port of the bad grid. The missing grid can then be replaced on the next filter cleaning.

ADVANTAGES

  • Small, compact, easy to carry in toolbox
  • Inexpensive, keep multiple units on-hand
  • Works on any DE filter, 24, 36, 48, 60 & 72 sq ft manifolds
  • Filter works normally with slightly less filtering square footage
  • Fits both top and older style bottom DE manifold filters
  • Finish the filter cleaning without delays
  • No need to waste time running to shop or a distributor for grids
  • Reusable, remove GridBlock on the next cleaning and reuse on another filter

Effects

  • Filtration area with GridBlock installed:
